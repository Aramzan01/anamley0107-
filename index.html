About Us

F-Star Renewable Energy Ltd is at the forefront of Mombasa’s transition to sustainable power, pioneering innovative solar and hybrid energy solutions. By harnessing the natural power of the sun and wind, we deliver clean, dependable energy that aligns with global climate goals while addressing local needs. Our transformative projects not only reduce environmental impact but also create meaningful employment, uplift small businesses, and strengthen vital infrastructure. Through green energy, we are helping Mombasa grow stronger, cleaner, and more self-reliant. At F-Star, we’re not just generating power—we’re lighting the way to a brighter, more resilient future.
